By using VirTech Academy's website and services, you agree to the data practices described in this policy. We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information — ever. Your trust is the foundation of everything we do.

01

Information We Collect

We collect information only when you directly interact with us — through registration, contact forms, or course activities. This includes:

  • Personal Details: Full name, email address, phone/WhatsApp number, and city.
  • Academic Background: Qualification level and employment status, collected during enrollment to recommend the right course.
  • Communication Data: Messages, questions, or feedback you send through our contact form, email, or Telegram group.
  • Usage Data: Automatically collected data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited — used only to improve website performance.
  • Payment Information: We do not store card or UPI details. All transactions are processed securely by our payment gateway partner (Razorpay or equivalent).
02

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to provide and improve our services. Here is exactly what we use it for:

Course Delivery

Sending class links, recordings, and study materials to enrolled students.

Updates & Notifications

Communicating batch updates, schedule changes, and new course launches.

Certificates

Issuing personalised completion certificates with your registered name.

Student Support

Responding to queries and providing ongoing academic and technical support.

03

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Your data stays with VirTech Academy, used only as described in this policy.

We may share limited data only in these specific situations:

  • Payment Processors: Razorpay handles your transaction data. We never see or store your card or UPI details.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by a court order, law enforcement, or governmental authority.
  • Service Tools: Trusted platforms such as Google Workspace, WhatsApp Business, or Zoom — used strictly to deliver our services.
04

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure:

  • All pages and data transmissions use HTTPS with SSL encryption.
  • Access to personal data is strictly limited to the four founding team members on a need-to-know basis.
  • Stored data is protected using secure, access-controlled cloud systems.
  • We conduct regular reviews of our data handling practices to maintain ongoing security.

While we take every reasonable precaution, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords and never share your login credentials with anyone.

05

Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your browser — to improve your experience. We use cookies for:

  • Session Management: Keeping you logged in during your current browsing session.
  • Analytics: Understanding which pages are most visited so we can improve content and usability.
  • Preferences: Remembering your settings for a smoother return visit.

You can disable or manage cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website.

06

Your Rights

You have full rights over your personal data. Contact us at any time to exercise any of the following:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to update any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request removal of your personal data from our systems (subject to legal record-keeping obligations).
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from promotional communications at any time by emailing us or clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send.
07

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external platforms such as YouTube, Telegram, LinkedIn, or partner resources. VirTech Academy is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We strongly encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information on those platforms.

08

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Significant updates will be announced through our Telegram community or by email. The updated date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
