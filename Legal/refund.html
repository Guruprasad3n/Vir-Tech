We built VirTech Academy on trust. This Refund Policy is designed to be fair, transparent, and student-friendly. Please read the full policy so you know exactly what to expect before and after enrollment.

01

Our 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you attend Week 1 and feel the course isn't right for you, contact us within 7 days of the course start date and we'll issue a 100% refund — no questions asked.

We are confident in what we teach, but we also understand that every learner is different. Our 7-day guarantee gives you the freedom to try the course risk-free and evaluate whether it's the right fit before committing fully.

This guarantee applies to the Complete Data Analytics Course and all individual module courses offered by VirTech Academy.

02

03

How to Request a Refund

The refund process is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps:

1
Send a Refund Request Email

Email us at info@virtech.academy with the subject line "Refund Request — [Your Full Name]". Include your registered email, phone number, and reason for the request.

2
We Acknowledge Within 24 Hours

Our team will confirm receipt of your request within 1 business day. We may ask a brief follow-up question to understand your experience better — this is for us to improve, not to delay your refund.

3
Verification

We verify your enrollment details and confirm your request falls within the eligible refund window or qualifies under another eligible scenario.

4
Refund Processed

Once approved, your refund is initiated within 2–3 business days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method within 7–10 business days depending on your bank.

04

Processing Timeline

Here's a clear breakdown of expected timelines at each stage of a refund:

  • Acknowledgement: Within 24 hours of your request.
  • Approval Decision: Within 2–3 business days of receiving your request.
  • Refund Initiation: Within 2–3 business days of approval.
  • Credit to Your Account: 5–10 business days after initiation, depending on your bank or payment method (UPI, debit card, credit card).

Refunds to UPI wallets are typically faster (2–3 days) than to debit or credit cards (up to 10 days). If you have not received your refund after 10 business days, please contact your bank before reaching out to us.

05

Batch Transfer Option

Can't attend your current batch? No problem. You can transfer your enrollment to the next available batch — completely free of charge.

To request a batch transfer:

  • Contact us at least 72 hours before your batch start date via WhatsApp or email.
  • Batch transfers are allowed once per enrollment. A second transfer request may be subject to a ₹200 administrative fee.
  • Transfers are subject to seat availability in the requested batch.
  • If no upcoming batch is available at the time, your enrollment will be held until the next batch is announced.
06

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • The refund request is submitted after 7 days from the course start date and the student has attended more than 2 weeks of classes.
  • The student was removed from the course due to a violation of the Code of Conduct or Terms of Service.
  • The student has downloaded, shared, or redistributed any course materials (recordings, slides, datasets, etc.).
  • The refund request is based solely on personal circumstances unrelated to course quality (e.g., schedule conflicts that existed before enrollment).
  • Discounted, promotional, or scholarship-based enrollments that were explicitly marked as non-refundable at the time of purchase.
07

Disputes

If your refund request is declined and you disagree with the decision, please follow this process:

  • Email us at info@virtech.academy with the subject line "Refund Dispute — [Your Name]" and explain your concern in detail.
  • A senior member of our founding team will personally review your case within 3 business days and respond with a final decision.
  • We are committed to resolving all disputes fairly and transparently. If you feel we have made an error, we want to know.

We ask that you contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank or payment provider. Filing a chargeback without first giving us the opportunity to resolve the issue may result in your account being permanently suspended.
