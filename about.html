Our Story

Building Data Skills for
Telugu Graduates

We are a passionate 4-member team on a mission to make industry-ready data analytics education accessible to every Telugu-speaking student — regardless of location or background.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, practical data analytics training in Telugu — making complex skills understandable, affordable, and directly applicable to real-world jobs for graduates across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

Our Vision

To become the #1 Telugu-language data education platform, helping 10,000+ students build career-changing skills over the next 3 years — bridging the language gap in tech education.

Our Values

Clarity over complexity. Telugu first, always. Practical over theoretical. Community over competition. Affordable access to quality education — these aren't slogans, they're our operating principles.

How We Started

Born from a Real Problem

We were four friends — engineers, marketers, and educators — who watched talented Telugu graduates struggle to break into data careers, not because they lacked intelligence, but because all quality content was in English.

We started recording Telugu explanations for Excel and SQL on a WhatsApp group. Within 3 weeks, the group had 200 members asking for more. That's when we knew VirTech Academy wasn't just an idea — it was a need.

Today we've trained 500+ students across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and even Telugu diaspora in the US and UK. Every class, every project, every doubt session — still in Telugu.
