Course Syllabus — 8 Weeks

From zero to a complete analytics portfolio in 8 structured weeks.

Week 1

Excel Basics

Formulas, functions, data formatting, and charts foundation.

Week 2

Advanced Excel & Dashboards

Pivot tables, VLOOKUP, conditional formatting, interactive dashboards.

Week 3

SQL Basics

SELECT, WHERE, GROUP BY, JOIN — querying real databases confidently.

Week 4

SQL Advanced

CTEs, Window functions, subqueries, and performance fundamentals.

Week 5

Power BI & Data Cleaning

Power Query, data modeling, relationships, and cleaning messy data.

Calculated columns, measures, KPI cards, and interactive reports.
